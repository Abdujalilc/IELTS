Frequency language  
- always → consistently, every time, without fail  
- usually → generally, typically, most of the time  
- often → frequently, regularly, commonly  
- sometimes → occasionally, now and then, from time to time  
- rarely → seldom, infrequently, hardly ever  
- never → not at all, under no circumstances, at no time  
- constantly → continually, repeatedly, nonstop  
- regularly → routinely, habitually, on a regular basis  
- occasionally → sporadically, intermittently, once in a while
