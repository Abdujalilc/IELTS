Structuring phrases  
- to begin with → firstly, initially, at the start  
- in other words → that is to say, to put it differently, namely  
- for instance → for example, such as, to illustrate  
- in particular → especially, specifically, notably  
- as mentioned earlier → as stated before, as noted above, as discussed previously  
- on the whole → overall, generally, in general  
- with regard to → concerning, regarding, in relation to  
- as far as X is concerned → with respect to X, in terms of X  
- in this case → here, in this situation, under these circumstances  
- to sum up → in summary, in conclusion, to conclude
