Degree modifiers:  
- extremely → highly, intensely, greatly  
- very → exceptionally, remarkably, noticeably  
- highly → strongly, deeply, exceedingly  
- fairly → moderately, reasonably, somewhat  
- quite → rather, somewhat, to some extent  
- slightly → marginally, a little, just a bit  
- barely → hardly, scarcely, narrowly  
- almost → nearly, virtually, practically  
- totally → completely, entirely, absolutely  
- significantly → considerably, notably, meaningfully
