Time references  
- in recent years → lately, over the past few years, in the past decade  
- currently → at present, as of now, nowadays  
- at the moment → for now, at present, temporarily  
- these days → nowadays, in modern times, today  
- over the past decade → in the last ten years, throughout the past decade  
- in the near future → soon, shortly, in the coming years  
- previously → earlier, before now, in the past  
- initially → at first, in the beginning, to start with  
- eventually → in the end, finally, after some time  
- recently → not long ago, just now, a short time ago
