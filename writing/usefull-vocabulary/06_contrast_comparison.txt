Contrast and comparison

1. Contrast words
- however
- although
- whereas
- while
- on the other hand
- in contrast
- nevertheless
- despite
- even though
- unlike

2. Comparison words
- similarly
- likewise
- both X and Y
- in the same way
- just as
- compared to
- in comparison with
- as...as
- like

3. Example phrases
- Although X increased, Y declined.
- While A rose steadily, B remained stable.
- In contrast to 2010, the figures in 2020 were much higher.
- X is similar to Y in terms of growth.
- Compared to last year, the profits doubled.
- Both cities experienced population growth.
