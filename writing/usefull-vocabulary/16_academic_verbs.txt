- imply → indicate, hint at, signal  
- demonstrate → show, prove, reveal  
- highlight → emphasize, stress, underline  
- emphasize → underline, draw attention to, focus on  
- contradict → challenge, oppose, disprove  
- evaluate → assess, judge, appraise  
- examine → investigate, study, explore  
- assert → claim, state, declare  
- establish → prove, determine, confirm  
- justify → defend, support, validate  
- define → describe, explain, identify  
- estimate → approximate, calculate, predict  
- influence → affect, shape, impact
