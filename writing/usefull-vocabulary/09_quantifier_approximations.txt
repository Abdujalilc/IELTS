Quantifiers and approximations

1. Large quantities
- a majority of
- most
- many
- a large number of
- numerous
- a great deal of
- a high percentage of

2. Small quantities
- a minority of
- few
- a small number of
- a small percentage of
- hardly any

3. Medium/uncertain quantities
- some
- several
- a portion of
- a certain number of
- part of

4. Approximations
- about
- approximately
- around
- nearly
- roughly
- just over
- just under
- close to

5. Example phrases
- Approximately 60% of students passed the exam.
- A large number of people commute by car.
- Only a small percentage of respondents agreed.
- Roughly half the population prefers online shopping.
- Some students found the test difficult.
